PredictIT - Crop Prediction

This project is an End-to-End Web Application designed for the Agriculture domain, leveraging Machine Learning and Generative AI. The application predicts optimal crop yields based on various factors such as soil type, weather conditions, and N,P,K values. We trained and tested three ML Models – Gaussian Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, and Random Forest. The RandomForest model outperformed with 99.8% accuracy, prompting us to integrate it into our solution. To further enhance precision, we also incorporated the all new Gemini Pro Generative AI.
